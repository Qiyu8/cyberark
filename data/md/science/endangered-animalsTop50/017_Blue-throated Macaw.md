# Blue-throated Macaw

![Blue-throated Macaw](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fd/Blue-throated_Macaw.jpg/800px-Blue-throated_Macaw.jpg)

**所属**: 玻利维亚

## 简介

蓝喉金刚鹦鹉（Ara glaucogularis），仅分布于玻利维亚中部，野生种群约250-300只，受非法捕捉宠物贸易严重威胁。
